Ugali recipe

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Corn flour
  • butter/blueband

Procedure

  1. Bring the water to a boil.
  2. Once steaming hot add a portion of the flour.
  3. Add the butter and stir till melted and intergrated.
  4. Stir the mix continuously until firm.
  5. Shape into a cylinder and leave on he heat for a moment.
  6. Serve with a stew of choice.
  7. Finally enjoy.
