Pilau recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • mixed spices
  • swahili spices
  • beef cubed
  • tomatoes
  • Garlic & ginger paste
  • onions
  • Chicken stalk
  • Vegetable oil

Procedure

  1. Wash your rice and rinse off excess starch.
  2. Chop the onions and tomatoes.
  3. fry your onions til golden brown.
  4. Add your garlic & ginger paste and stir.
  5. Shape into a cylinder and leave on he heat for a moment.
  6. Add your tomatoes and fry into a paste then add your beef.
  7. Fry till beef is well cooked.
  8. Add an appropriate amount of water and bring to a boil.
  9. Add your rice and boil
  10. Serve with a salad or ovacado
