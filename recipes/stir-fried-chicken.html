Stir-fried-chicken recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken diced
  • Vegetable oil
  • herbs
  • lemons
  • onions
  • garlic & ginger
  • butter & thyme

Procedure

  1. Bring the chicken to a slight boil and then drench.
  2. Fry your onions ill golden brown add your ginger & garlic paste.
  3. Stir together thn add the lemon skin shavings.
  4. Add the chicken and stir fry for a while.
  5. Once golden brown add butter and thyme and baste.
  6. Add lemon juice to the mixture and boil down .
  7. Finally best served with ugali and enjoy.
